# Evolution of CPU-GPU Architectures: Towards AI-Driven Heterogeneous Computing Systems

> A comprehensive survey on the evolution of computing architectures from traditional CPU-centric systems to modern AI-driven heterogeneous computing platforms.

---

## 📖 Overview

This repository contains the research paper, datasets, Python scripts, and visualizations developed as part of my Master's research in **High Performance Computing (HPC) / Quantum Computing** at **Technische Hochschule Deggendorf, Germany**.

The paper explores how computing architectures have evolved to meet the growing computational demands of Artificial Intelligence (AI), High-Performance Computing (HPC), and Large Language Models (LLMs).

---

## 🎯 Research Objectives

- Study the evolution of traditional CPU architectures
- Analyze the emergence of GPUs as parallel computing accelerators
- Explore Heterogeneous System Architecture (HSA)
- Investigate modern AI-driven heterogeneous systems
- Compare current AI accelerators from NVIDIA, AMD, and Intel
- Discuss future directions including Chiplets, HBM, CXL, NPUs, and AI-native architectures
